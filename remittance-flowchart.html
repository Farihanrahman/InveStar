Detailed Process Steps

1. Initiation (USA)

User in the USA opens the InveStar app and initiates a remittance transfer. They enter the amount in USD and provide recipient details in Bangladesh.

2. Quote Generation

The InveStar API requests a real-time quote from MoneyGram for USD to BDT conversion, including fees and exchange rates.

3. User Confirmation

User reviews the quote, confirms the transaction, and provides recipient information (name, phone, pickup location).

4. Blockchain Transaction

StellarService converts USD to USDC on the Stellar blockchain and transfers it to the service/anchor wallet for processing.

5. MoneyGram Integration

MoneyGramService creates a payout transaction through the MoneyGram API, specifying the recipient and pickup location in Bangladesh.

6. Processing

MoneyGram processes the payout transaction and converts the USDC to BDT for local distribution.

7. Webhook Notification

MoneyGram sends a webhook notification to InveStar confirming the transaction status and completion.

8. Recipient Notification (Bangladesh)

The recipient in Bangladesh receives notification that funds are available for pickup at the specified MoneyGram location.

9. Cash Pickup

Recipient visits the MoneyGram location in Bangladesh, provides identification, and collects the BDT cash.
