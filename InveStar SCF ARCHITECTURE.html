Section 1 · Page 2

System Overview

Why Stellar — not Ethereum or Solana: MoneyGram Ramps (the SCF Integration List building block) runs exclusively on Stellar SEP-24/10. Bangladesh Bank's USDC prohibition is solved by the SEP-12/24 compliance stack paired with MoneyGram's fiat conversion infrastructure. Stellar's $0.001 tx fee is the only way to hit ~1% all-in cost on a $50–200 transfer. The Soroban programmable investment layer is Stellar-native — no equivalent exists elsewhere for this corridor.
